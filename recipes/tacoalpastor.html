Taco Al Pastor

Description:Tacos al Pastor are a classic street food made from thin slices of pork marinated in a blend of chilies and pineapple, then cooked on a spit and served on corn tortillas with onion, cilantro, and salsa.

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder (thinly sliced)
  • Pineapple slices
  • Dried guajillo chiles
  • Garlic cloves
  • Vinegar
  • Cumin
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Corn tortillas
  • Chopped onions
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges

Steps:

  1. Soak and blend chilies with garlic, vinegar, and spices to make marinade.
  2. Marinate pork overnight.
  3. Stack meat and pineapple slices and cook (ideally on a vertical spit or grill).
  4. Slice cooked meat thinly.
  5. Serve on tortillas with onion, cilantro, and lime.
