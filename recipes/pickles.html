Pickles

Description:Crisp, tangy cucumber pickles made with a simple vinegar brine and fresh dill. Perfect for burgers, sandwiches, or snacking.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 small cucumbers (Kirby or pickling cucumbers work best)
  • 1 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds (optional)
  • Fresh dill sprigs

Steps:

  1. Prep the cucumbers: Wash and cut cucumbers into spears or round slices.
  2. Make the brine: In a small saucepan, combine vinegar, water, salt, and sugar. Heat and stir until salt and sugar dissolve. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  3. Fill the jar: In a clean jar, layer the cucumbers with garlic, peppercorns, mustard seeds, and dill.
  4. Add the brine: Pour the warm brine over the cucumbers until fully submerged.
